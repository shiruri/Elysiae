Using Elysiae

1 What is Elysiae?

Elysiae is a hospital management system that helps you manage patients, doctors, appointments, wards, admissions, departments, billing, and medical records — all from one place.

2 Your Role

What you can do depends on your assigned role. Administrators have full access to all features. Doctors can manage their schedules and view patients. Patients can book appointments and view records. Receptionists handle front-desk tasks like searching records and managing appointments. Nurses assist with ward and admission operations.

3 Navigation

Sidebar — On desktop, hover over the left sidebar to see full labels. Click any item to go to that section.

Mobile — Tap the menu icon (three lines) at the top-left to open the sidebar. A bottom navigation bar also provides quick access to the most-used sections.

Keyboard shortcuts — Press G then a letter to jump to any section. Press ? to see all shortcuts. See the Shortcuts tab for the full list.

4 Key Features

Dashboard — Quick overview of hospital activity at a glance.
Appointments — Schedule, view, and manage appointments. Filter by date, doctor, or patient.
Patients — Search and manage patient records. View history and generate invoices.
Doctors — Manage doctor profiles, departments, and schedules.
EHR Records — View and manage electronic health records for patients.
Wards & Beds — Manage hospital wards and track bed availability.
Admissions — Admit, discharge, and transfer patients between wards and beds.
Departments — Organize and manage hospital departments.
Billing — Track invoices and payments.
Reports — Generate summary reports for hospital operations.
Staff — Manage user accounts and assign roles.

5 Deleting Records

When you delete a patient, doctor, department, ward, bed, or user account, it is not permanently removed. Instead, it is hidden from lists and searches. This prevents accidental data loss and keeps related records intact. If you need to recover a deleted record, contact your system administrator.

6 Accessibility

Elysiae includes accessibility features to make the system easier to use. Access them from the floating button (bottom-left corner) or the Accessibility tab above:

  • Adjust font size (4 levels)
  • High contrast mode for better visibility
  • Readable font option for dyslexia support
  • Bold text and increased spacing
  • Reduce motion if animations are distracting
  • Focus mode to highlight the active element
  • Reading guide line that follows your cursor
  • Color filters (grayscale, reduced saturation)
  • All settings are saved automatically

7 Need Help?

If you encounter any issues or have questions, contact your system administrator or IT support team. They can assist with account issues, access permissions, and data recovery.
